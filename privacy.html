Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

What MuteUI does

MuteUI is a Chrome extension that hides comments, posts, and words on social media websites based on rules you set. It also validates license keys for premium access.

Data that stays on your device

All of your settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Muted keywords and accounts per platform
  • WebReplace rules
  • Toggle states and theme preferences
  • A randomly generated device ID (used only for license validation)

None of this data is sent to us, shared with anyone, or used for analytics.

Data sent to our server

When you activate a license key or when the extension checks your subscription status, two pieces of information are sent to our API server:

  • Your license key
  • Your device ID (a random string, not tied to your identity)

The server responds with whether the key is valid. That's it. No browsing history, no muted words, no account names, no page content.

Data stored on our server

If you purchase a subscription through Stripe, our server stores:

  • The email address you provided at checkout
  • Your generated license key
  • Device IDs registered to that key (up to 3)
  • Subscription status from Stripe

We do not store passwords, payment details, browsing data, or anything related to what you mute or filter.

Stripe

Payments are handled by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing: stripe.com/privacy

Third parties

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party for any reason. We do not run ads. We do not use analytics or tracking scripts in the extension or on this website.

Cookies

This website and the extension do not use cookies.

Your rights

You can delete all your local data by uninstalling the extension. If you want your server-side data removed (license key, email, device IDs), email us and we'll delete it.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, reach out at the support page on the Chrome Web Store listing.
